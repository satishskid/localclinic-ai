AI Medical Assistant

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

KEY POINT: All data processing occurs locally on your device. No medical data, personal information, or analysis results are ever transmitted to external servers.

Overview

AI Medical Assistant ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our browser extension handles your data.

Data Collection and Processing

What We DO NOT Collect

  • No Personal Health Information (PHI) is collected or transmitted
  • No medical records are stored on external servers
  • No patient data leaves your device
  • No browsing history is tracked or collected
  • No analytics or tracking data is sent to third parties
  • No account registration is required
  • No cookies are used for tracking purposes

What Stays on Your Device

  • AI Models: Downloaded once and cached locally in your browser
  • Analysis Results: Processed and displayed locally, never transmitted
  • User Preferences: Stored in browser's local storage only
  • Report Drafts: Saved locally until you choose to export them

How the Extension Works

Local AI Processing

  • Text/Image Analysis: AI model runs entirely within your browser using WebAssembly
  • No Server Communication: Analysis requests never leave your device
  • Model Caching: AI models downloaded once from public CDN (Hugging Face) and cached locally

Browser Permissions

The extension requests minimal permissions required for functionality:

  • activeTab: Access current tab content only when you click the extension
  • storage: Save your preferences locally in browser storage
  • contextMenus: Add right-click menu for quick analysis
  • scripting: Extract selected text/images for local analysis

HIPAA Compliance

Our privacy-first architecture supports HIPAA compliance:

  • No PHI Transmission: Protected health information never leaves your device
  • No BAA Required: Since we don't process or store any PHI, no Business Associate Agreement is needed
  • Local Processing: All analysis occurs on your machine
  • No Data Retention: We don't retain any medical or personal data

Your Rights

  • Data Deletion: Clear browser data or uninstall extension to remove all local data
  • Data Access: All your data is already on your device - you have full access
  • Data Portability: Export reports in standard formats anytime
